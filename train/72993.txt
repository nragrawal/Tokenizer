

साचा:Infobox Indian AwardsThe Lalit Kala Akademi Fellowship, also, Lalit Kala Akademi Ratna is an honour for the fine arts in India, given to eminent artists for their life time achievements in the field of visual arts. It is awarded by the Lalit Kala Akademi, India's National Academy of Art, it is the highest honour in the fine arts conferred by the Government of India. 
The ‘Fellow,’ carries a purse money of Rs.25,000/-, a citation and a plaque. The Fellow is honoured with a portfolio of his/her art works. The first elected fellow, in 1955, was the artist Jamini Roy.
