

Madras Talkies is an established Film and Television Production and Distribution Company in India setup in 1995 with Mani Ratnam and Suhasini Maniratnam as partners. Madras Talkies is actively involved in production of films and television serials, which are distributed all over the world. The company has produced over 9 feature films and 6 television serials till date.
Ratnam served as a producer on several films before setting up the banner in 1997. The company is located in Madras, Tamil Nadu.
