Sabudanyachi khir
Sahitya:sabudana 1 wati dudhsakharvelchi
Kruti:1. Sabudana 2 wati panayat ukala2. sabudana shijayala laglyawar tyat dudh ghala3. satat dhavalat raha4. sabudana phuglya war tyat sakhar taka ani changale dhavalun ghya5. Jar ghatta hot aasel tar tyat ankhi dudh misala6. sakhar virghalyawar gas banda karun tyat velchi pud taka7. hi khir faar garam aasate, tyamule thodi komat zalya war khanyas dyavi.8. hi khir pachnyas farach halki aasate.
