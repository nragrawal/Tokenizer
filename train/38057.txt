Wikimania is a conference held yearly for users of the Wikimedia Foundation projects, including Wikipedia, Wikinews, etc. 
The first conference was held in Frankfurt, Germany, on August 4-8, 2005. The second conference was held in Boston, USA, the third one was held in Taipei, Taiwan and the fourth Wikimania was held in Alexandria, Egypt .
It has been decided by the jury team that Wikimania 2009 will be held in Buenos Aires.
